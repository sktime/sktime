# dataset_forecasting Wishlist

Wishlist of forecasting datasets requested for inclusion in sktime.

## How to Add Items

1. Open an issue on the [sktime issue tracker](https://github.com/sktime/sktime/issues) describing the dataset
2. Add the item below following the format template
3. Submit a PR with your addition

## Format Template

```markdown
- [ ] **Dataset Name** - Brief description
  - Source: [Link](link)
  - Issue: #1234
```

## Wishlist Items

- [ ] **M1, M2, M3, M4, M5 competition datasets** - Standard forecasting competition benchmarks with loaders.
- [ ] **M6 financial forecasting** - Financial series benchmark from the M6 competition.
- [ ] **Tourism forecasting dataset** - Tourism arrivals/time series forecasting benchmark.
- [ ] **Electricity load datasets (GEFCom2012, ElectricityLoadDiagrams)** - Electricity demand and load datasets.
- [ ] **Traffic forecasting datasets (PeMS, Uber Movement)** - Road and urban traffic volume datasets.
- [ ] **Financial time series (stocks, forex, cryptocurrencies)** - Market time series benchmarks.
- [ ] **Weather/climate datasets (NOAA, ERA5, Berkeley Earth)** - Meteorological and climate series for forecasting.
- [ ] **Energy consumption datasets (UK smart meter, Pecan Street)** - Household and building energy usage.
- [ ] **Wikipedia page views** - Web traffic forecasting datasets.
- [ ] **Kaggle forecasting competitions (Store Sales, Web Traffic)** - Kaggle forecasting benchmarks with loaders.
- [ ] **Ride-hailing demand datasets** - Demand and supply time series (e.g., taxis, ride-hailing).
- [ ] **Retail sales datasets (Walmart, Rossmann)** - Multi-series retail sales time series.
- [ ] **Call center volume datasets** - Call arrival and workload forecasting datasets.
- [ ] **Hospital admission forecasting** - Healthcare demand time series datasets.
- [ ] **Air quality forecasting (PM2.5, ozone)** - Environmental pollutant forecasting datasets.
- [ ] **Solar/wind power generation** - Renewable energy generation series.
- [ ] **GDP and economic indicators** - Macroeconomic time series datasets.
- [ ] **Commodity prices** - Time series of commodity spot and futures prices.
- [ ] **Airline passenger datasets** - Passenger count series (classical and extended).
- [ ] **COVID-19 time series** - Case, hospitalization, and related time series.
- [ ] **Network traffic datasets** - Internet and network load series.
- [ ] **Server load datasets** - CPU/memory/load monitoring time series.
- [ ] **IoT sensor streams** - Multivariate sensor forecasting datasets.
