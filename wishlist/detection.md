# Detector Wishlist

The following algorithms have been requested by the community but are not yet implemented in `sktime`:

* momentfm anomaly detection foundation model - [#9500](https://github.com/sktime/sktime/issues/9500)
* HampelFilter anomaly detector - [#9478](https://github.com/sktime/sktime/issues/9478)
* Kernel-MMD (Maximum Mean Discrepancy) change point detector - [#9475](https://github.com/sktime/sktime/issues/9475)
* QuoVadisTAD baselines and metrics for time series anomaly detection - [#7650](https://github.com/sktime/sktime/issues/7650)
